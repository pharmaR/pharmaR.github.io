At a recent ShinyGatherings, we held a workshop for the {riskassessment} application, focusing on how we can simplify R package validation with a user-friendly approach that can be tailored to your specific risk assessment requirements. We appreciate Appsilon for the opportunity to speak about our app!



About the Workshop

The main topics of the workshop included:

  • What the {riskassessment} app is and how it simplifies the risk assessment process with a structured and user-friendly approach without the need to use individual scripts alone

  • Why you should use the app and how it assists with making informed decisions about packages

  • How the app guides end-user workflows, from logging into the app, to reviewing package metrics and checking dependencies, to justifying package requests and submitting the request

  • How to customize the app with custom risk assessment rules, decision automation, role-based access control, and storage of organization-specific settings

  • Why the app stands out in 7 big ways, namely its automated package assessments, comprehensive risk summary reports, and more

  • Common challenges we recognize related to the app
